Private by design.

Cledger was built with privacy first. Your work history, earnings and personal records stay on your device and remain entirely under your control.

No accounts. No subscriptions. No hidden data collection.

Everything Cledger does is designed to keep your information private, local and under your control.

  • Fully Offline

    Use Cledger without an internet connection. Your records are processed and stored directly on your device.

  • Stored on Your Device

    Your work history, earnings, expenses and reports remain on your phone or tablet.

  • No Account Required

    No registration, email address, profile or login is needed.

  • No Tracking

    No advertising trackers, behavioural analytics or background monitoring.

  • No Data Selling

    Your information is never sold, shared with data brokers or used for advertising.

  • Backups You Control

    You decide when to create a backup, where to save it and when to restore it.
