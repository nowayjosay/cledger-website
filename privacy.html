Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 16, 2026  ·  Last Updated: July 16, 2026

Operated by: Hamben  ·  Website: cledgerapp.com  ·  Contact: cledgerapp@gmail.com

1. Introduction

Welcome to Cledger ("the App", "we", "our", or "us"). Cledger is a personal income and work-day tracking application designed for offshore and maritime workers. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, where it is stored, and your rights regarding that information.

By downloading and using Cledger, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

Cledger allows you to voluntarily enter the following types of personal and financial information for your own record-keeping purposes:

2.1 Personal Profile Information

  • Full name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Home address
  • Home country
  • Tax ID or Social Security Number (SSN)

2.2 Work Entry Records

  • Work dates
  • Day rate (earnings per day)
  • Company name
  • Country of work
  • Job position / title
  • Notes
  • Payment status (paid or unpaid)
  • Payment date

2.3 Tax Day Records

  • Tax day dates
  • Country
  • Notes

2.4 Expense Records

  • Expense dates
  • Amount
  • Category
  • Country
  • Description

2.5 Yearly Targets

  • Target working days
  • Target earnings
  • Number of rotations

All of the above information is entered by you voluntarily. You choose what to enter. You are not required to fill in every field.

3. How We Use Your Information

All data you enter into Cledger is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To display your financial dashboard, earnings summaries, and progress toward your yearly targets within the App
  • To generate charts and statistics (work-life balance, payment turnaround, yearly overview, etc.)
  • To produce backup files that you can save to your device for safekeeping
  • To restore your data from a backup file you provide

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the App's features for you.

4. Where Your Data Is Stored

ALL data entered into Cledger is stored exclusively on your device using SQLite, a local on-device database.

  • No data is transmitted to any external server
  • No data is stored in the cloud
  • No data is shared with Hamben or any third party
  • The App does not require an internet connection to function

Your data never leaves your device unless you manually export it yourself using the backup feature described in Section 5.

5. Backup and Data Export

Cledger includes a manual backup feature that allows you to export your data as a CSV (comma-separated values) file.

  • On iOS: The App uses the system Share Sheet to allow you to save the backup file to your Files app, email it to yourself, or share it via any app you choose.
  • On Android: The App requests permission to write to your chosen storage folder and saves the backup file there.

You are fully in control of where your backup goes. Hamben never receives, accesses, or stores your backup files.

You may also restore data by uploading a previously saved backup CSV file. This replaces your current on-device data with the data from the file.

6. Device Permissions

Cledger requests the following device permissions:

  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (Android only): Required to allow you to select a backup file from your device storage for restore.
  • WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (Android only): Required to allow you to save a backup file to your chosen folder on your device.

These permissions are used only for the backup and restore features. They are not used to scan, access, or collect any other files on your device.

Cledger does NOT request or use: location / GPS, camera, microphone, contacts, calendar, or any permission beyond what is listed above.

7. Third-Party Services

Cledger does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising networks, crash reporting tools, or data collection SDKs. No third party receives any data from the App.

8. Children's Privacy

Cledger is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has entered personal information into the App, you can delete all data using the "Clear All Data" feature in Settings, or contact us at cledgerapp@gmail.com.

9. Your Rights and Data Control

Because all your data is stored locally on your device, you have full control at all times. You may at any time:

  • View all your data within the App
  • Edit or delete any individual entry
  • Use the "Clear All Data" feature in Settings to permanently delete all data stored by the App on your device (work entries, tax entries, expenses, yearly targets, and personal profile)
  • Uninstall the App, which removes the App's database from your device

If you have any questions about your data or wish to contact us regarding privacy, email us at: cledgerapp@gmail.com

10. Data Security

Because your data never leaves your device, the security of your information depends on the security of your phone. We recommend:

  • Using a screen lock (PIN, password, or biometric) on your device
  • Keeping your device's operating system up to date
  • Storing backup files in a secure location

Hamben is not responsible for unauthorized access to your device or to backup files you have saved or shared.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
