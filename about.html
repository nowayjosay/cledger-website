Built to solve a problem
we knew too well.

After years of juggling spreadsheets, notes and old records, we believed there had to be a better way.

A personal project born from real offshore experience and designed to bring work history, earnings and career records into one private place.

Founder

As an offshore professional, I spent years looking for a simple and private way to keep track of my work history, earnings and career records, but nothing truly fit the way rotational professionals work. Cledger started as a personal project to solve my own frustrations and eventually became an app that I wanted to share with others who live and work in a similar way.

Looking ahead

Cledger will continue to evolve with new tools and improvements while staying true to the principles it was built on: privacy, simplicity and giving professionals better visibility over their work and finances.
